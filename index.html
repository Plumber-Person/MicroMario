About Plumber Person for Pebble

"Everything you love about Super Mario Bros, now on the Pebble Watch!"

The application we are showing here is a port of Super Mario Bros made to work on the Pebble Watch.

Using the e-ink screen from the Watch, and the provided buttons, as a team, we have created a working port of our favourite game.

This app was created as part of the 2015 HACKBU Hackathon at Binghamton University.

History of our Project:

  • March 20, 2015

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Three people came together, first as individuals, then as a team. We had several ideas, but settled on porting Super Mario Bros over to the Pebble Watch.

  • March 21, 2015

    An Application is Born!

    After a few hours of video games (hey, you have to test out the game before you port it), we sat down to flesh out the program and code. Drinking a few too many Monster drinks, we finally had our code working and a new UI that would interface with the watch well!

  • Late Evening of March 21, 2015

    Transition to Finished Product

    Putting finishing touches on the code, trying all the functions, and making everything perfect!

  • March 22, 2015

    Hackathon Ends, Fun Begins

    We rushed to the finish, but made it. It is now time to bring it to the world!
